## TCL CSOT at SID Display Week 2024 - a 1000 Hz 4K monitor, 85&quot; UD w-HVA Pro TV display, and more

## 19 May 2024, 13:14:40
Add DisplaySpecifications as a preferred source on GoogleTCL CSOT attended the SID Display Week 2024 showcasing several groundbreaking products. Two of those grabbed the prestigious People's Choice Awards:

The 85" 8K Flat Pro display features the first 4-mask process and the largest 1G1D display size for an 8K LCD screen, with rapid 5ms GTG response time, and a 120Hz refresh rate
The world's first 14" Inkjet Printing QD-EL Display has a 2.8K resolution, a 30-120Hz VRR refresh rate, and surpasses BT2020's color gamut by over 85%. It also has a significantly reduced cost thanks to high-efficiency QD materials for printing and a simplified manufacturing process.


TCL CSOT has also introduced various impressive display solutions:

The 85'' UD w-HVA Pro is the latest flagship 85'' TV display panel designed to rival OLED displays in performance and quality. It features super-wide-angle HVA (w-HVA) technology for consistent image quality at a CESI viewing angle of 178° and a refresh rate of up to 144Hz. With a low reflection rate of 1.4%, the impact of ambient lights is minimized, offering an immersive viewing experience ideal for sports and high-frame-rate movies, even in bright indoor environments.
The 4K 1000 Hz monitor display is engineered with TCL CSOT's proprietary P2P national standard protocol (CSPI) and ensures unmatched performance and responsiveness. It also delivers industry-leading 6 Gbps transmission and adaptive equalizer technology.
The CR 4000:1 HFS monitor display has a 4000:1 contrast ratio and a wide color gamut covering DCI-P3 99%. It also arrives with a four-sided borderless design with an ultra-slim bezel of 4 mm.
The 57" 8K MLED monitor display offers 8K resolution, a 1000R curvature, and a super-high color gamut. The product delivers a peak brightness of 1600 nits and VESA DisplayHDR 1400 compatibility. With over 11,000 local dimming zones, it yields OLED-like contrast ratios and its "Halo S-grade" performance, as well as Freesync Premium Pro compatibility ensuring a lifelike and immersive gaming experience.


Other exhibited TCL CSOT display solutions included a 16" 8K Laptop Display with 100% sRGB, the world's first Ln-Oxide 14" MUX 1:3 Display, the world's first 7.85" Tandem Trifold Screen, a 14" 2.8K Inkjet Printing Hybrid OLED Display with a 120Hz refresh rate, and the world's first 7.85" Under Screen Face Auth Display.
