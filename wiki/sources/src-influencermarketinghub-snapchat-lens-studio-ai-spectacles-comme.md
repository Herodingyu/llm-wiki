# Snapchat Unveils Lens Studio AI, Spectacles Commerce, and AR Gaming Upgrades at Lens Fest 2025

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/influencermarketinghub-snapchat-lens-studio-ai-spectacles-comme.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

At Lens Fest 2025, Snap announced major ecosystem upgrades including Lens Studio AI for conversational AR creation, Commerce Kit for in-Lens payments, Spectacles OS 2.0 with WebXR support, and Snap Cloud backend infrastructure. The platform has grown to 400,000+ developers and 4 million community-created Lenses.

## 关键要点

- Lens Studio AI enables AR creation through natural language prompts and conversation
- Blocks Framework introduces modular, reusable AR components for collaborative development
- Commerce Kit allows creators to sell digital goods and premium AR features inside Lenses
- Spectacles OS 2.0 features Travel Mode (stable AR tracking in motion) and EyeConnect (shared Lenses via gaze)
- Snap Cloud built with Supabase provides backend for real-time, privacy-safe AR experiences
- 175 million users play Lens Games monthly (130% increase YoY)
- Spectacles public launch targeted for 2026

## 产品/技术细节

- **Lens Studio AI**: Conversational assistant for building AR experiences, writes code, debugs, generates assets
- **Blocks Framework**: Reusable AR modules (physics, lighting, 3D textures) shareable across developers
- **Spectacles OS 2.0**: WebXR support, Travel Mode, EyeConnect, Commerce Kit integration
- **Snap Cloud**: Secure storage, APIs, edge computing, real-time processing
- **Graphics upgrades**: StyleGen (realistic textures), FaceGen (facial geometry), AI Clips (animated video Lenses)
- **Gaming**: Chat-integrated Games Drawer, Bitmoji tools, live multiplayer matchmaking coming 2026
