## Top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world








November 19, 2024

With the Mini LED market scale soaring, Mini LED chip market competition is fierce, the upstream chip manufacturers to accelerate the industrial layout, expand the industrial scale to meet market demand. Mini LED driver chip is a semiconductor device used to control Mini LED backlight. For example, the installation of Mini LED driver chip small digital display, the chip by receiving control signals from the main control chip, enhance the driving ability of these signals, and output to the backlight control chip. The backlight control chip enhances the control signals and then outputs them to the Mini LED backlight driver chip, which in turn drives the many pixels of the Mini LED to emit the target color and brightness, thus presenting the image as a whole. This article will introduce you top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world.




## Top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world





## 1. Macroblock









## Official website: https://www.mblock.com.cn/cn






Macroblock has launched the MBI5762 and MBI5780 common-negative driver chips. MBI5762 product features include 48-channel, up to 32-sweep design, can support 240 frames of source input, the application of point spacing across the P4-P0.8mm, the display effect can be up to 16-bit grayscale, 7680Hz refresh rate. MBI5762 is equipped with super visual computing function to alleviate the common low gray flickering and scan line problems during shooting. MBI5780 is a common-cathode driver chip designed for ultra-small-pitch (0.4mm-1.2mm) displays, featuring current accuracy control of less than +/-1%, 20 built-in MOSFET, and support for 90-sweep line columns in one. These three features enable the MBI5780 to be widely used in Mini/Micro LED displays. In addition, MBI5780 introduces the second generation of bad point isolation function, which effectively prevents visual effect problems such as LED open, cross, short-circuit caterpillar and so on.




## 2. WH





## Official website: http://www.whsmc.com.tw/






WH has launched the Mini LED backlight display driver products WH5097D, WH3803D, WH5436 and WH8166M. The WH5097D active matrix driver, a 4-channel LED driver, is designed to drive locally dimmable displays. It supports 12-bit PWM and can also realize direct drive or scanning type drive method. And up to 30mA continuous current can be provided on each channel. The WH3803D is a dedicated driver for three-channel RGB LED circuits. The chip internally includes intelligent digital ports, data latches, and signal shaping amplification driver circuits. It also contains a high precision internal oscillator and a 15V high voltage programmable constant current output driver. The WH5436 is dedicated to low dropout, high precision 16-bit grayscale RGB LED constant current driver chip for LED display. It can generate grayscale according to the input data, and it is easier to form deep grayscale and display high-quality picture. It is characterized by point correction and high gray level PWM control. With an optical module thickness of &lt; 0.4mm, the WH8166M can be used in LEDFALD backlighting systems, and also supports hybrid LED driver solutions.




## 3. Samsung









## Official website: https://www.samsung.com/






Samsung has introduced two new Mini LED driver chips, S6LP441 and S6LDMB1. These two are essential for improving the luminous efficiency of Mini LED. The S6LP441 is a Mini LED backlight driver chip. S6LDMB1 is the backlight control chip, responsible for monitoring the operation of S6LP441. The S6LP441 is a Mini LED current driver, placed next to the Mini LED so that it can be better controlled.




## 4. Xm-Plus





## Official website: https://www.xm-plus.com/page-57806.html






Founded in August 2017 and headquartered in Xiamen, Xm-Plus is a professional top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturer in the world. In the LED field, Xm-Plus mainly focuses on the research and development of innovative driver architectures, advanced color algorithms, and high-speed data transmission interfaces for Mini/Micro LED, providing perfect solutions including highly integrated driver chips supporting high resolution, timing controllers with built-in color processing engines, and peripheral power chips. Xm-Plus has released two Mini class LED display driver chips with common-cathode structure, XM11202G and XM10720G respectively. The XM11202G and XM10720G are both independently developed by Xm-Plus and can be used in P0.3 to P1.8 Mini and Micro LED indoor display applications, and have already been certified and mass-produced by a number of top 10 LED manufacturers in the world.




## 5. NOVATEK









## Official website: https://www.novatek.com.tw/zh-CN/Home/index






Founded in 1997, NOVATEK is one of the top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world. NOVATEK main products include a full range of flat panel display driver chips, as well as digital audio/video and multimedia chips for mobile terminals and consumer electronics products. In addition, NOVATEK occupies a leading position in the smartphone TDDI chip market. According to the latest news, NOVATEK plans to start mass production of OLED displays using TDDI (Touch and Display Driver Integration) technology as early as the second quarter of 2025.




## 6. TLi









## Official website: https://www.tliled.com/






TLi, one of the Top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world, is a Korean fabless semiconductor company. TLi is a Korean fabless semiconductor company that designs timers and LED driver ICs (key semiconductor devices for display panels). Unlike other brands, it is able to reduce the number of driver chips used for the same hardware requirements. TLi has released a common cathode driver IC, TL3103UD, a common cathode product that significantly reduces power consumption compared to common anode.




## 7. TI





## Official website: https://www.ti.com.cn/






TI announced a Mini LED driver chip TLC6983. TLC6983 is a highly integrated Mini LED display driver chip with 48 constant current sources and 16 scanning FET. A single TLC6983 is capable of driving 16×16 RGB LED pixels, and two TLC6983s stacked at the same time can drive 32×32 RGB LED pixels. To achieve low power consumption, the device supports separate power supplies for red, green, and blue LED through its common cathode structure. In addition, the TLC6983 operates at such a low power that it can operate normally at a low voltage and current of 2.5V operation and 3.9mA operation.




## 8. SILERGY









## Official website: https://www.silergy.com/






SILERGY released a high efficiency, high precision and high integration Mini LED driver chip SY7780. With 11-bit global LED current setting, 13-bit independent PWM time setting and 8-bit independent LED current setting, SY7780 perfectly solves the problems of insufficient picture brightness, ghosting, and uneven low grayscale, which will occur in traditional chips. The independent 8-bit local current setting can realize brightness compensation and improve display uniformity. SY7780 is mainly used in VR glasses, tablet PCs, laptops and other Mini/Micro LED displays.




## 9. HYASIC





## Official website: https://www.huayuansemi.com/






HYASIC has launched an AEC-Q100 automotive grade AM Mini LED driver chip, HY8602B0QA. HY8602B0QA is an 8-channel AM Mini LED driver for car play screen, which supports 11bit DC dimming and 12bit PWM dimming. It can support up to 23bit hybrid dimming and has a single channel 30V/48mA driving capability. The HY8602B0QA utilizes HYASIC proprietary SPB (Serial-Parallel-Broadcast) patent protocol. SPB patent has excellent communication reliability, supports up to 16MHz communication frequency, and can realize single-sided wiring. It supports up to 16MHz communication frequency and single-sided cabling. Its powerful fault detection mechanism ensures system stability and supports long-distance transmission, providing users with efficient and reliable communication solutions.




## 10. SUNMOON









## Official website: http://www.chinaasic.com/






As one of the top 10 Mini LED driver chip manufacturers in the world, SUNMOON has successively launched Mini LED backlight PM driver SM6610, Mini LED backlight AM driver SM6212 series and so on. SM6610 is a 48-channel constant current full array local dimming driver chip for Mini LED backlighting. It supports up to 8 sweep applications, and a single chip can control up to 384 Mini LED dimming partitions. SM6212 series is a 12-channel constant current, single-channel maximum output 40mA current static drive local dimming chip, suitable for MINI LED backlighting field.
