# 2026: Rising at Dawn, Forging a New Path — JBD

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/jb-display-newsdetails-85html.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

JBD's 2025 year-in-review highlights major milestones including reducing defective pixels to ≤3 per panel, launching Hummingbird II and Roadrunner I Polychrome Projectors, deploying ARTCs-WG on RayNeo's production line, and completing Series B2 financing exceeding RMB 1 billion. By end of 2025, JBD MicroLED products were integrated into nearly 50 smart glasses models.

## 关键要点

- Defective pixels reduced from ≤100 to ≤3 per panel (May 2025)
- ARTCs-WG core equipment deployed on RayNeo production line (June 2025)
- Series B1 financing completed (August 2025); Series B2 exceeded RMB 1 billion (October 2025)
- Strategic collaboration with Applied Materials and RayNeo for full-color waveguide AR glasses (September 2025)
- Hummingbird II Polychrome Projector launched: 0.2cc volume, CES 2026 Innovation Awards Honoree
- Roadrunner I Polychrome Projector launched: 2.5μm pixel pitch (December 2025)
- Nearly 50 smart glasses models integrated with JBD MicroLED by end of 2025

## 产品/技术细节

- **Hummingbird II**: 0.2cc ultra-compact volume, full-color display, CES 2026 Innovation Awards Honoree
- **Roadrunner I**: 2.5μm pixel pitch microdisplay panel, superior size/weight advantage
- **ARTCs-WG**: Core equipment of JBD's ARTCs image quality engine, deployed on RayNeo line
- **Partners**: Applied Materials, RayNeo for full-color optical waveguide AR glasses
- **Customers**: Alibaba Quark AI Glasses, RayNeo X3 Pro, INMO GO3, LLVision Leion Hey2, Xuanjing M5
