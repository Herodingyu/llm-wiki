# I just tested the futuristic Rokid Glasses — bringing AR and AI together

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/tomsguide-computing-smart-glasses-i-just-tested-th.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Rokid Glasses at IFA 2025 represent one of the first commercially available AR/AI glasses combining dual waveguide displays with on-device AI. At just 49g and $599 MSRP, they undercut expected pricing from Meta and Google while offering features like live translation, ChatGPT-5 multimodal interaction, and turn-by-turn navigation.

## 关键要点

- Rokid Glasses weigh just 49g (1.7 ounces), positioned as world's lightest AR/AI glasses
- Dual monochrome green Micro LED waveguide displays, 480×398 per eye, 23° FoV, 1,500 nits
- Powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon AR1 Gen 1 chip
- 12MP camera with viewfinder overlay for better photo framing
- Live language translation with subtitles at bottom of display
- ChatGPT-5 multimodal interaction through onboard camera
- MSRP $599, significantly below expected Meta Ray-Ban Display pricing (~$1,000 rumored)

## 产品/技术细节

- **Display**: Dual monochrome green Micro LED waveguide, 480×398 resolution per eye, 1,500 nits brightness, 23-degree FoV
- **Chip**: Qualcomm Snapdragon AR1 Gen 1
- **Camera**: 12MP F2.25 aperture
- **Audio**: Dual directional speakers, 4-mic array
- **Battery**: 210mAh with fast charge support
- **Connectivity**: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3
- **Weight**: 1.7 ounces (49g)
- **Price**: $599 MSRP
