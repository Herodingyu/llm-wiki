# AI Smart Glasses Consumer Market Research Report 2034

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/marketintelo-report-ai-smart-glasses-consumer-market.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

The global AI smart glasses consumer market was valued at $9.2 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach $62.8 billion by 2034 at a 24.5% CAGR. AI-powered no-display glasses hold the largest share (38.4%), but AR smart glasses are the fastest-growing segment at 28.3% CAGR. Meta leads with Ray-Ban sales exceeding 2 million units.

## 关键要点

- Market size: $9.2B (2025) → $62.8B (2034), CAGR 24.5%
- Technology segments: No-display AI glasses 38.4%, AR glasses 31.2%, MR headsets 19.8%, HUD glasses 10.6%
- Regional: North America 38.2%, Asia Pacific fastest-growing at 27.1% CAGR
- Meta (Ray-Ban) leads with 2M+ units sold; top 5 players account for ~71% of revenue
- Entry-level AI audio glasses now retail below $299; premium AR models converge at $799-$1,499
- Over 52 million consumer AI smart glass units projected to ship annually by 2028

## 产品/技术细节

- **Meta Ray-Ban**: 2M+ cumulative units, 100M+ AI queries/month by Q3 2025
- **Qualcomm Snapdragon AR2 Gen 2**: 12 TOPS on-device AI, announced late 2025
- **Samsung Galaxy Glasses**: Previewed at MWC 2025, 58g, $799 target, Gemini AI
- **XREAL Air 2 Ultra**: 72g, 52° FoV, $420M estimated 2025 revenue
- **Distribution**: DTC online 43.6%, retail 31.8%, carrier bundles 14.7% (fastest-growing at 29.3% CAGR)
