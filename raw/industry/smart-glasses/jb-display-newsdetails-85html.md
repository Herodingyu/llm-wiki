## 2026: Rising at Dawn, Forging a New Path



Date：2026.01.01
Source：JBD




Reflecting on 2025, it was a pivotal year in which JBD continued to fortify its core MicroLED microdisplay technology and accelerate commercial product deployment. Throughout the year, we spearheaded advancements across multiple dimensions: in display quality, we established a rigorous new industry benchmark by reducing the defective pixel count to &le;3 per panel; in product evolution, we unveiled two flagship innovations&mdash;the Hummingbird II and Roadrunner I Polychrome Projectors; and in advancing system integration, the core equipment of JBD&rsquo;s ARTCs image quality engine, the ARTCs-WG, was successfully deployed on the RayNeo production line. Furthermore, we partnered with Applied Materials and RayNeo to redefine the visual fidelity of full-color optical waveguide AR glasses.

In 2025, the landscape of AR/AI smart glasses diversified rapidly, with deepening exploration into new applications. Our customers introduced a range of innovative products, including the Alibaba Quark AI Glasses, RayNeo X3 Pro, INMO GO3, LLVision Leion Hey2, and Xuanjing M5. By the end of 2025, JBD MicroLED microdisplay products had been integrated into nearly 50 smart glasses models. This burgeoning product ecosystem serves as a powerful testament to the application potential of MicroLED in lightweight AR near-eye display scenarios.

In 2026, JBD will &quot;Forge a New Path.&quot; Centered on the new generation 2.5&mu;m pixel pitch MicroLED platform &quot;Roadrunner,&quot; we will continue to drive product exploration while simultaneously expanding MicroLED into new application horizons, advancing our strategic layout for the next phase of growth.

&nbsp;

&nbsp;2026 Highlights Review&nbsp;

Setting a New Benchmark in Defective Pixel Control

In May 2025, JBD achieved a significant breakthrough by reducing the number of defective pixels in MicroLED microdisplays from &le;100 to &le;3 per panel, thereby establishing a rigorous new benchmark for defective pixel metrics across the industry.

Commercial Deployment of the ARTCs

In June 2025, the ARTCs-WG, serving as the core equipment of JBD&rsquo;s ARTCs image quality engine, was successfully deployed on the RayNeo production line.

Completion of Series B1 Financing

In August 2025, the company successfully completed its Series B1 financing round, raising hundreds of millions of RMB.

Breaking Barriers in AR Image Quality

September 2025 marked a strategic collaboration as JBD partnered with Applied Materials and RayNeo to significantly enhance the display fidelity of full-color optical waveguide AR glasses.

Hummingbird II Polychrome Projector

Also in September 2025, JBD officially launched the &quot;Hummingbird II&quot; Polychrome Projector. Boasting an ultra-compact volume of just 0.2 cc, this innovation unlocks a pristine full-color display experience for lightweight AR glasses. Notably, the product was also named a CES Innovation Awards&reg; 2026 Honoree.

Completion of Series B2 Financing

In October 2025, JBD completed its Series B2 financing round exceeding RMB 1 billion, setting a new record for a single funding round in the global MicroLED microdisplay sector.

Roadrunner I Polychrome Projector

In December 2025, JBD officially launched the &quot;Roadrunner&quot; Polychrome Projector. Featuring a 2.5&mu;m pixel pitch microdisplay panel, it demonstrates a compelling advantage in size and weight while elevating the visual experience to unprecedented heights.

&nbsp;

&nbsp;2026 Forging a New Path&nbsp;

Looking toward 2026, JBD will continue to anchor its strategy in MicroLED microdisplays, driving the synergistic evolution of technology, products, and applications.

In the realm of near-eye displays, the &quot;Roadrunner&quot; Polychrome Projector, based on the 2.5&mu;m pixel pitch platform, will serve as the new starting point for exploration. By continuously optimizing luminous efficiency and manufacturing yields, JBD will further consolidate its advantage in technology and product leadership.

Ultimately, &ldquo;Forging a New Path&rdquo; signifies more than breakthroughs at the cutting edge&mdash;it embodies the relentless pursuit of new application frontiers. As we deepen our stronghold in AR near-eye displays, JBD is poised to project its core MicroLED capabilities into vast new horizons, unlocking scalable innovation across a limitless spectrum of future scenarios.
