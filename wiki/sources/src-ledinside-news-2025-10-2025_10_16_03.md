# JBD Launches the "Roadrunner" Platform: MicroLED Microdisplays with 2.5-μm Pixel Pitch

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/ledinside-news-2025-10-2025_10_16_03.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

JBD announced its next-generation "Roadrunner" MicroLED microdisplay platform featuring a 2.5-μm pixel pitch, achieving 10,160 PPI pixel density. The platform reduces polychrome projector package volume by over 50% compared to the previous Hummingbird generation while capping backplane power at 18mW using a 22nm silicon process.

## 关键要点

- "Roadrunner" features 2.5-μm pixel pitch (down from 4-μm in Hummingbird)
- Pixel density reaches 10,160 PPI — 2.56x pixel count at equivalent display area
- First adoption of 22nm-node silicon backplane, backplane power capped at 18mW
- Package volume reduced by >50% for polychrome projector at same resolution
- Already deployed in nearly 50 AR smart-glasses models including Rokid, Alibaba Quark, RayNeo X3 Pro
- Mass production anticipated in second half of next year

## 产品/技术细节

- **Pixel pitch**: 2.5 μm (vs 4 μm Hummingbird)
- **Pixel density**: 10,160 PPI
- **Backplane**: 22nm-node silicon process, 18mW power
- **Volume reduction**: >50% for equivalent resolution polychrome projector
- **Deployment**: Nearly 50 AR smart-glasses models
- **Mass production**: Phased rollout anticipated H2 next year
