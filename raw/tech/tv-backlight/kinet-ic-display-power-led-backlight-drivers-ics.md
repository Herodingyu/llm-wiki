## LED Backlight Drivers ICs
You are here: Home / Display Power / LED Backlight Drivers ICs











LED backlight driver ICs control the voltage and current for white LEDs used to illuminate LCD panels. Typical applications include portable appliances such as Smartphones, Tablets, Cameras, Notebook PCs as well as displays such as Computer Monitor and TV.  Kinetic Technologies’ portfolio is one of the largest in the industry and features high efficiency operation with high accuracy at low dimming duty cycles. To enhance reliability, products are designed with several protection mechanisms including, but not limited to, LED short circuit, LED open circuit and over-current.
