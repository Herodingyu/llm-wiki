# I Just Saw the Future of AR Glasses, and This Feature May Fix the Biggest Problem

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/tomsguide-computing-smart-glasses-i-just-saw-the-f.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Qualcomm partnered with Belgian deep-tech startup VoxelSensors to integrate Single Photon Active Event Sensor (SPAES) 3D sensing technology with Snapdragon XR platforms. The collaboration targets up to 10x power savings and lower latency for XR applications, aiming to enable all-day wearable AR smart glasses.

## 关键要点

- Qualcomm and VoxelSensors partner to optimize SPAES 3D sensing with Snapdragon XR platforms
- SPAES delivers up to 10x power savings with lower latency for XR apps
- Targets enabling all-day wearable AR smart glasses with spatial computing
- Advances Physical AI — interpreting the world from the user's point of view
- Expected availability on Snapdragon AR2 Gen 1 by December 2025

## 产品/技术细节

- **SPAES technology**: Single Photon Active Event Sensor for 3D sensing
- **Platform**: Snapdragon XR chips (AR2 Gen 1 initially)
- **Key benefits**: Up to 10x power savings, lower latency, reliable depth sensing under challenging lighting
- **Physical AI**: Processes data from human perspective to help machines understand environments
- **Use cases**: Product recognition, real-time navigation, contextual AR overlays
