# How AI And Connected Workflows Will Close The Verification Bottleneck

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/tech/soc-pm/semiengineering-how-ai-and-connected-workflows-will-clos.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Siemens EDA 首席验证科学家 Harry Foster 撰写的文章，讨论了 AI 和互联工作流如何解决芯片开发中的验证瓶颈问题。文章介绍了 Verification Productivity Gap 2.0 的概念及其解决方案。

## 关键要点

- 验证已成为芯片开发周期的瓶颈，验证周期增长速度超过设计周期
- 仅 14% 的 ASIC 项目实现首次硅片成功，创历史最低记录
- 传统验证依赖孤立的点工具，导致冗余测试、手动调试和覆盖可见性碎片化
- AI 驱动的智能测试生成、预测性回归分析和辅助调试正在改变验证流程

## 关键引用

- "Verification has become the bottleneck in chip development cycles, with verification cycle growth exceeding design cycle growth."
- "Only 14% of ASIC projects achieve first silicon success, representing a historic low."
- "Traditional verification relies on isolated point tools, leading to redundant testing, manual debugging, and fragmented coverage visibility."
- "AI-driven intelligent test generation, predictive regression analysis, and assisted debugging are transforming the verification workflow."

## 技术细节

- Verification Productivity Gap 2.0：由 3D IC、Chiplet 设计和软件定义架构驱动
- AI 验证功能：智能测试生成、预测性回归分析、AI 辅助调试
- 互联验证工作流统一仿真、仿真和硬件/软件协同验证
- 单次流片成本在先进节点可超过 1000 万美元
