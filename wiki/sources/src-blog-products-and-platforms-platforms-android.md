# Galaxy XR: The first Android XR headset

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/blog-products-and-platforms-platforms-android.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Google's official blog post introducing Galaxy XR as the first Android XR headset. The device represents a collaboration between Google and Samsung to create a new XR platform powered by Android and Gemini AI, bringing immersive experiences to headsets and laying groundwork for future glasses.

## 关键要点

- Galaxy XR is the first headset running Android XR
- Co-developed by Google and Samsung
- Powered by Gemini AI for intelligent assistance
- Part of Google's broader Android XR ecosystem strategy
- Represents Google's re-entry into XR with a platform approach

## 产品/技术细节

- **Platform**: Android XR
- **AI**: Gemini integration
- **Form factor**: Headset (first phase), glasses roadmap
- **Ecosystem**: Android app compatibility, Play Store integration
