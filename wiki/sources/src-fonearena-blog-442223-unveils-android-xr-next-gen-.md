# Google unveils Android XR for next-gen extended reality devices

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/fonearena-blog-442223-unveils-android-xr-next-gen-.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Google introduced Android XR, a new operating system for next-generation XR headsets and glasses, developed in collaboration with Samsung. The platform integrates Gemini AI, supports ARCore, Unity, and OpenXR, and will first launch on Samsung's "Project Moohan" headset. Google is also partnering with Lynx, Sony, and XREAL for a wide range of devices.

## 关键要点

- Android XR is Google's new OS for XR headsets and glasses, co-developed with Samsung
- First headset: Samsung "Project Moohan" available next year
- Gemini AI integration for guidance, task planning, and real-time information
- Developer tools: ARCore, Android Studio, Jetpack Compose, Unity, OpenXR
- Hardware partnerships: Lynx, Sony, XREAL, Magic Leap
- Supports mobile and tablet apps from Google Play
- Android XR SDK and Jetpack XR SDK launched with developer preview

## 产品/技术细节

- **Platform**: Android XR built on open standards (OpenXR 1.1)
- **AI**: Gemini assistant integrated at system level
- **SDK features**: Spatialized apps, immersive interaction (hand/eye tracking), familiar tools (Kotlin, Unity, C/C++)
- **Jetpack XR**: Compose for XR, Material Design for XR, SceneCore, ARCore
- **Web support**: Chrome supports WebXR with Three.js and A-Frame
- **Google apps**: YouTube, Google TV, Google Photos (3D), Google Maps (Immersive View), Chrome (multiple virtual screens)
