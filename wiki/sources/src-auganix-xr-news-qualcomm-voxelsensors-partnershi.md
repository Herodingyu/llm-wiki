# Qualcomm and VoxelSensors Partner to Optimize 3D Sensing

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/auganix-xr-news-qualcomm-voxelsensors-partnershi.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

VoxelSensors announced a collaboration with Qualcomm to optimize its Single Photon Active Event Sensor (SPAES) 3D sensing technology with Snapdragon XR platforms. The integration targets up to 10x power savings and reduced latency, with optimized solutions expected to be available to select customers by December 2025.

## 关键要点

- VoxelSensors SPAES technology to be integrated with Snapdragon AR2 Gen 1 Platform
- Delivers up to 10x power savings and reduced latency for depth sensing
- Maintains performance across varied lighting conditions
- Supports Physical AI — processing data from human perspective
- Expected availability to select customers and partners by December 2025
- Targets enabling all-day wearable AR smart glasses

## 产品/技术细节

- **SPAES**: Single Photon Active Event Sensor for 3D sensing
- **Platform integration**: Snapdragon AR2 Gen 1
- **Power savings**: Up to 10x
- **Use cases**: 3D sensing, eye-tracking, egocentric data capture for AI agents
- **Availability**: December 2025 for select customers
