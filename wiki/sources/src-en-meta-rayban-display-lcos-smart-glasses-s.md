# Meta Launches 'Ray-Ban Display' with LCoS Smart Glasses, Showcases PIC-Based LCoS at SID 2025

## 来源

- **原始文件**: raw/industry/smart-glasses/en-meta-rayban-display-lcos-smart-glasses-s.md
- **提取日期**: 2026-05-02

## 摘要

Meta announced the Meta Ray-Ban Display smart glasses at Meta Connect 2025, featuring a monocular LCoS display with Lumus waveguide. Simultaneously, Meta Reality Labs showcased research on photonic integrated circuit (PIC)-based ultra-compact laser microdisplays at SID 2025, potentially shrinking AR light engines to less than 1 cm³.

## 关键要点

- Meta Ray-Ban Display: monocular display at bottom of right lens
- Uses OmniVision single-panel full-color LCoS: 600×600 resolution, 42 PPD, 20° monocular FoV, up to 5,000 nits
- Combined with Lumus waveguide for outdoor visibility
- Meta chose LCoS over green LEDoS based on technology maturity, power efficiency, and full-color capability
- At SID 2025, Meta showcased PIC-based ultra-compact laser microdisplay research
- PICs replace PBS, focusing lenses, and dichroic mirrors with on-chip optical functions
- LEDoS not expected to be fully competitive until 2028+

## 产品/技术细节

- **Display**: OmniVision single-panel full-color LCoS, 600×600, 42 PPD, 20° FoV, 5,000 nits
- **Waveguide**: Lumus waveguide
- **Positioning**: Intermediate step between Ray-Ban AI glasses and Orion AR glasses
- **PIC-based LCoS research**: Potential to shrink light engines to <1 cm³, 50° viewing angle, high color uniformity
- **LCoS competitors**: Himax Display, Avegant, Raontec developing next-gen high-brightness engines
